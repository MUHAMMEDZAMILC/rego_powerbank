About Us

Welcome to Rego, the forefront of smart and fully automated power bank rental services. Our mission is to keep you connected, powered up, and on the go without the hassle of a dead battery. With our innovative network of internet-connected vending terminals, accessing a power bank has never been easier.

At Rego, we understand the importance of staying charged in today’s fast-paced world. That’s why we’ve developed a cutting-edge system that allows you to rent power banks effortlessly. Our terminals are strategically located, ensuring that a power source is always within your reach.

How It Works

Step 1

Download APP and Navigate to the nearest Plugo

Step 2

Scan the QR Code to release a power bank

Step 3

Carry the power bank with you to charge your smart device

Step 4

Simply drop the power bank in the nearest Plugo terminal to return

Goals & Objective

At Rego, our primary goal is to ensure uninterrupted connectivity by providing a reliable and accessible power source. We aim to expand our network of vending terminals across the UAE, making it easy to rent a power bank wherever you are. We prioritize delivering a superior user experience through our intuitive app and efficient rental process, constantly innovating to meet our users' needs. Our objectives include enhancing the Rego APP, engaging with customers for feedback, and promoting eco-friendly practices like recycling old power banks and using energy-efficient technologies. By focusing on these goals, Rego is dedicated to ensuring you stay powered up and connected wherever you go.
